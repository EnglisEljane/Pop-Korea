JAY PARK

The Fresh Prince of Seoul

Famously known for his stage name Jay Park, Park Jae Beom is singer-songwriter, rapper dancer and actor. He was born in the US, but later move to Korea to be in JYP entertainment. He joined the Kpop group 2PM with the debut song "10 out of 10". In 2010, he left his group, and became a solo artist. In 2011, he released his 1st mini album,"Take a deeper look".

ERIC NAM

Nam Yoon Do

Also born in the US, Eric Nam is a solo artist in the korean music industry. He is a singer-songwriter, telelvision host and entertainer. He debuted in 2013 with the title track "Heaven's Door". In 2014, he released his first digital single "Ooh Ooh", in 2015 he released another one called "I'm Ok" and in 2016 he released "Can't help myself."

IU

Nation's Little Sister

Lee Ji Eun, widely known in Korea as IU, is solo artist under LOEN entertainment. She was born in Seoul. She failed an audition in JYP but was accepted to LOEN. In 2008, she release her first album "Lost and Found". During 2011, she was casted in a series called Dream High along with Suzy. She released a new song called Palette in 2017.


TRIPLEH

Hui, Hyuna, E'Dawn

Triple H is a newly debuted kpop group trio under the CUBE entertainment. It is composed of Hyuna, Hui and E'Dawn from pentagon. On May 1, 2017, they released a mini album called 199X. They also released a music video called 365 FRESH. Hyuna is a former member of 4Minute while Hui and E'Dawn are currently in a kpop group called Pentagon. Triple H is Cube's 2nd Troublemaker.

CL

CL-roo

Lee Chae-rin,stage name CL, is a former member of 2NE1. She was the leader, main rapper and Lead vocalist of the group. On October 2014, she had her US debut. On the year 2016, YG announced that 2Ne1 is going to be disbanded. She still continued with her solo career and made her first ever solo tour in the US. She released 2 songs of her own called Hello B*tches and Lifted.

ZICO

Woo Ji Ho

Zico is a member of Block B, a kpop group under Stardom Entertainment. The group debuted on the year 2011 and in 2013, left their agency and signed with Seven Seasons. He is the leader, main rapper, and vocalist of Block B. In 2014, he released his official solo debut called "Tough Cookie". In 2015, he released Boys and Girls and on 2017, he release She's a Baby.


HYUNA

Kim Hyun Ah

HyunA is a solo artist signed under CUBE entertainment. She is a former member of 4minute and the Wondergirls. She was originally an idol under JYP but in 2008 she transferred to CUBE entertainment. She was the face of 4minute, and was the only member who renewed her contract. She had many hit songs like Bubble Pop, Red,How's This and Roll Deep. Currently, she is in a trio called Triple H.

AKMU

Akdong Musician

AKMU or Akdung Musician is a duo under YG entertainment. This duo is Chanyuk and Soohyun, brother and sister that both won in Kpop Star Season 2. Lee Chan Hyuk is the rapper and composer while her sister, Lee Soo Hyun is the main vocalist of the group. Actually, they are both main vocalists. They released their first song titled "I Love you" in 2013. In 2014, they released the songs 200%, Melted and Give Love.

TAEYEON

Kim Tae Yeon

Taeyeon is the leader and main vocalist of the famous girl group, SNSD or Girl's Generation. She was casted by SM entertainment in 2004 by winning the SM 8th Annual Best Contest as Best singer. SNSD debuted n 2007. And since 2012, Taeyeon was a part of the sub-unit group called TTS. Even though she is a member of SNSD, she also has a solo career. In 2015 she released her album called "I".
